Privacy Policy.

Last Updated: June 15, 2024 v1.2.0

ShopFlowPOS is built with a "Privacy-by-Design" architecture. We prioritize the security of your business intelligence by ensuring that your sensitive business data stays exactly where it belongs: locally on your device.

1. Introduction & Scope

Matech Matrix ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how ShopFlowPOS ("the App") handles your data. This policy applies to all users of the ShopFlowPOS mobile application and desktop software.

Our philosophy is simple: Your business data is none of our business. We have engineered this App to be an offline-first solution that grants you total ownership of your information.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

To provide maximum security for mobile shop owners, ShopFlowPOS does NOT collect, store, or have access to any of the following:

  • Business Records: We do not see your inventory, sales, costs, or profits.
  • Sensitive Identifiers: We do not collect IMEIs, serial numbers, or device IDs of the products you sell.
  • Customer Information: We do not have access to your customer names, phone numbers, or ledger histories.
  • Location Data: We do not track the physical location of your store or your movements.

3. Information Collection & Usage

We only collect the minimum amount of information necessary to maintain account security and app stability:

3.1 Account Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication (a Google service) to manage your account. When you sign up, we only store your email address. This is required to identify your license and allow you to reset your password.

3.2 Analytics & Technical Performance

To ensure 99.9% operational uptime, we use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics. This data is fully anonymized and aggregated. It includes technical details such as:

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • App version and build number.
  • Anonymized technical stack traces for app crashes.
  • Frequency of feature usage (e.g., how often the "POS" screen is opened).

Note: No financial data or business-specific information is included in these technical reports.

4. Local Storage & Security

All your business data is stored in a secure local database (SQLite) on your device. We use industry-standard encryption protocols (AES-256) to protect sensitive database fields.

Because this data is local, it never travels over the internet unless you explicitly initiate a backup or sync.

5. User-Controlled Backups (Cloud Sync)

ShopFlowPOS offers an optional Cloud Sync feature to prevent data loss. If enabled, the App uses your Google Drive API to store an encrypted backup file on YOUR OWN Google Drive.

Matech Matrix does not have the keys to access these files, nor can we view the content of these backups. You maintain 100% control and ownership of your backup files.

Your Responsibility

As an offline-first App, the responsibility for data retention lies entirely with you. We strongly recommend performing daily backups. If you lose your device or uninstall the App without a backup, your data may be permanently lost. We cannot recover it for you.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party infrastructure to facilitate app operations:

  • Google Play Services: For app updates and license management.
  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and technical stability monitoring.
  • Google Drive API: For user-initiated backups (optional).

These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. We will only disclose information if required by law to comply with a legal obligation or to protect the safety of our users.

8. Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption at rest for local databases and secure SSL/TLS protocols for authentication traffic.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data (the email used for login), including the right to access, correct, or delete it. You can manage your account directly within the App settings or by contacting us.
